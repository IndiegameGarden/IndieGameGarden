
*** Building a new IGG client for Portable App distribution or In-Program Updatable distribution
- set    GardenConfig.IS_INSTALLER_VERSION = false
- update GardenConfig.IGG_CLIENT_VERSION (one higher than last release)
- update GardenConfig.KNOWN_GAMELIB_VERSION and check if equal to gamelib-config.json "GameLibVer".
- run build.bat
